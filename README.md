
# 🎵 Taynections

**Taynections** is a Taylor Swift-themed word association game inspired by the popular *NYT Games: Connections*. Put your Swiftie knowledge to the ultimate test by grouping 16 songs into four sets of four based on the album they belong to.

With **100 curated rounds**, this game spans Taylor’s entire discography—from the country roots of *Debut* to the poetic depths of her latest eras.

## 🕹️ How to Play

The objective is simple but the challenge is "Enchanted":
1. **Find the Groups:** Identify four songs that share a common Taylor Swift album.
2. **Select & Submit:** Click on four tiles and hit "Submit" to see if your connection is correct.
3. **Watch Your Mistakes:** You only have **4 mistakes** allowed per game before it's "Game Over."
4. **Clear the Board:** Successfully categorize all 16 songs to win the round!

## ✨ Features

* **100 Unique Rounds:** A massive library of puzzles that get progressively trickier.
* **Discography-Wide:** Includes tracks from *Taylor Swift*, *Fearless*, *Speak Now*, *Red*, *1989*, *reputation*, *Lover*, *folklore*, *evermore*, *Midnights*, and *The Tortured Poets Department*.
* **Intuitive UI:** Clean, responsive design featuring the iconic "Eras" purple palette.
* **Shuffle & Reset:** Stuck? Shuffle the board to see the songs from a "New Perspective."
